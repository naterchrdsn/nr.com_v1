More About Me:

Nate Richardson is a Web developer specializing in object-oriented programming and front-end Web development. He is an IT Consultant with a proven track record of helping make people’s lives and businesses easier to manage. With wife Chelsea, Nate is co-owner of a creative firm that is a powerful combination of their multi-industry specialties and experience.

Nate brings six years of experience and a penchant for the details of minimalist design, as well as a decade of experience building and maintaining all types of computers. He attained an Associate in Arts for Web Development & Design from the Harrisburg Area Community College in May of 2014, and has been on the Dean’s List every semester.

Described as ambitious and passionate, Nate excels at optimizing technology and developing code aimed at helping others do what they love, better.
